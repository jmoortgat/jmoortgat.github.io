Prospective Students & Postdocs

I am always interested in hearing from motivated PhD students and postdoctoral researchers with strong backgrounds in machine learning, computer vision, remote sensing, or computational geosciences. My group works at the intersection of AI and Earth observation — if you are excited about applying deep learning to satellite data to address real environmental and energy challenges, I would love to hear from you.

Current openings are particularly in: coastal bathymetry via physics-informed AI, satellite-based monitoring of deforestation and agroforestry in Africa, and computational modeling of natural hydrogen reservoirs. Please send me an email with your CV, a brief description of your research interests, and relevant code samples or publications.
